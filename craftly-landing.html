The Process

Four steps. Zero cloud dependency.

From question to action — everything happens locally, on your network, with your data never leaving Bangladesh.

Step 01

You Ask

Type your question in natural language. Bangla, English, or mixed — Craftly understands context.

Voice input and Bangla keyboard support coming soon.

Step 02

Agent Thinks

The AI agent plans a multi-step action — not just a text response, a real workflow.

Powered by a locally fine-tuned LLM for Bangladeshi context.

Step 03

Mesh Searches

Your query hits the local device mesh. Nearby nodes contribute inference power privately.

Zero raw data is shared — only encrypted inference tokens.

Step 04

Action Delivered

Real results: a blood donor found, a document translated, a connection made. Not just words.

The Mission

Bangladesh does not need to wait for permission to build the future.

I — Hunger

We have more to prove.

Every great technology era was driven by people who had nothing to lose and everything to build. Bangladesh's young engineers carry that same fire — the kind that turns late nights into breakthroughs. That urgency is not a disadvantage. It is our engine.

II — Scale

170 million minds in the loop.

A model trained on Bangladeshi language, culture, and lived experience does not exist yet. But it needs to. Craftly Robot is that model — and every young person who joins the team adds a perspective Silicon Valley cannot replicate or purchase.

III — Ownership

We build it. We own it.

For too long, the tools that shape our country's information flow were built elsewhere, tuned for other languages, other problems. Craftly is Bangladesh's first attempt to own the full stack — from inference hardware to the LLM itself. That changes everything.

"A generation that builds its own LLM does not just create software — it decides how its language is understood, how its problems are solved, and how its future is imagined. That is a form of sovereignty."
— Craftly Team, Dhaka, 2025
